Roingus is an experimental project deployed as a collection of interconnected smart contracts on Fantom blockchain.

The living heart of Roingus project is ERC20 Roingus Token (ticker $RNGS).

Deployed as a mutable proxy contract, RNGS is free to change and adapt to new upgrades from the developers. All other contracts will be deployed as proxies as well, to help facilitate any errors or bugs Roingus community may encounter on it's way.

The vision of the Roingus is a DAO powered by the holders of RNGS token, that controls Treasury and all subdivision contracts. Those contracts utilize RNGS token for various endeavours, inluding on-chain games, novel distribution mechanics and many more.

Our goal is to encourage pushing smart contracts paradigm into unforseen realms, not just blatantly copypasting farms or other degeneracy, but to try to invent something new and exciting.

All the upgrades to smart contracts will be shown on Log homepage and the DAO interface will be available soon.
